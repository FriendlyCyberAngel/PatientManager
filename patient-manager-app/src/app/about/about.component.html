About Patient Manager

This is a healthcare system that will help you manage patient recommendations. The system has a few sections that will allow you to have access to the data of the patients in the system.

First of all, login into the system so you can properly access the features of the system.

After that, click on the patient section to access the data of the patients. In this section, a list of patients will be displayed, click on a patient in the list and you will be able to see his recommendations.

In the recommendations section, you will be able to manage all the patient recommendations.
